Join Us

  • If you are interested in joining any of our research projects, please get in touch. We have opportunities for students (both graduate and undergraduate).

  • Graduate Students

    Graduate students can join the lab through a number of graduate programs at IIT. Those available with the department are

    • Master of Science in Information Architecture (IARC)
    • Master of Science in Technical Communication and Information Design (TCID)
    • Doctor of Philosophy in Technical Communication (TECH)

  • Undergraduate Students

    We hire undergraduate students as research assistants and programmers. If you are interested in either kind of position, please check the ResearchWeb listings for the most current info.
